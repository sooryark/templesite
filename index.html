Arulmigu Alli Thurai Sri Kamakshi Amman Koodipattu Temple

The Arulmigu Alli Thurai Sri Kamakshi Amman Koodipattu Temple is one of the most revered shrines in Tamil Nadu. It is known for its divine grace and vibrant spiritual ambiance. This sacred temple has been a cornerstone of faith for devotees for generations, offering solace and blessings to all who visit.
